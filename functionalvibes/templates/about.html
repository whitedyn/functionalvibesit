As you are reading these words, you are probably sitting down, slouched somewhere with your head tilted forward. And chances are that the time you spend in this position during the day, every day, is quite long. If this sounds familiar, your body has probably already adapted to a new, dysfunctional setting. You could already be feeling some pain or discomfort in your lower back or neck.

Here is where we come into the story. Our team of highly qualified Coaches, specialised in functional re-education, is the best choice for you to:

  1. Restore the functionality of your body, making it stronger and more resilient
  2. Maximise the effectiveness of your training and
  3. Learn how to exercise on your own

Unlike what happens in the majority of personal training centres or fitness club, here at Functional Vibes each Coach follows a common approach to human movement which is grounded in scientific evidence and 10+ years of clinical experience.

Functional Vibes, however, is not only about professional expertise and technical innovation.
